HRMS, or Human Resource Management System, is a software solution that helps businesses manage various aspects of HR operations, including employee data, payroll, benefits administration, recruitment, performance management, and more. By centralizing HR processes and data, HRMS enables organizations to automate tasks, improve efficiency, and enhance decision-making.
